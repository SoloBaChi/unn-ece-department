Alumni Intervention in the Department of Electronic Engineering, University of Nigeria

Ever since the formation of the Department of Electronic Engineering, UNN in 1981, our prestigious students turned Alumni have contributed immensely in the improvement and facilitation of projects to help the department in its goal of being the universities ‘Center of Excellence’. Their goodwill have not gone unnoticed and we wish to thank them for their exceptional contributions.
