Department of Electronic Engineering

Faculty Of Engineering

University of Nigeria, Nsukka

Enugu State, Nigeria

Brief Profile

I currently occupy the position Senior Lecturer in the Department of Electronic Engineering, University of Nigeria where I joined in 2004 prior to that I worked in the industry at various positions. I have a strong educational and technical background with extensive experience in teaching and researching in the field of Digital Electronics and ICT. My personal goal is to constantly cultivate an understanding of ICT and ways it can provide value to the growth of society.
