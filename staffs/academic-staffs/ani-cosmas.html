About Prof. Ani I. Cosmas (Ph.D)

Professor, Ani Cosmas Ikechukwu is a professor of Electronic Engineering, at the department of Electronic Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, University of Nigeria, Nsukka. He began work at the department after the completion of his M.sc degree programme in automated telecommunication from the former soviet union in 1986. His interested research areas Telecommunications Networks Performance management and resource management. He is an Associate member of the Institute of Electrical Engineers (AMISE), Member of Nigeria Society of Engineers (MNSE) and The Engineering Council Register (United Kingdom). Prof. Ani Cosmas is the author/coauthor of several books including: “Electrical Transmission Media & Antenna’s”, FIAC press Enugu, Nigeria, 2010. And “Beginners Choice for Learning Programming (C++ and Java)”, St. CIKO publishers, 2010. He has held several leadership positions in the department of Electronic Engineering and faculty of Engineering including the Head of Department, Electronic Engineering and the Associate Deanship of the Faculty of Engineering.
